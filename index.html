    • Hello, my name is Lorenzo Salvio and I am currently the Software Developer Lead at Wela in Atlanta, Georgia. I began this position in January of 2019 shortly after completing my 16 weeks at the DigitalCrafts Immersive Bootcamp in Atlanta with the September '18 cohort. I am a 22 year old, originally from California. I first began coding one year ago with a hobby course on Udemy. At the time, I was attempting to break into the L.A. music scene as a DJ, and I wanted to build myself a website. Coding for the sake of coding quickly took over, and music production became the hobby. In the spring of 2018, I decided to prioritize studying coding full time, and stopped pursuing my Bachelor's degree in Business at California State U, Fullerton. I moved to Atlanta to attend DigitalCrafts and I can say with full confidence that it was the right decision. Since I began my journey with programming and development, I have fallen in love with everything that I have done. For the first time in my life, I feel like an eager student every day. When I'm not coding, I enjoy having deep discussions with friends, writing music, and reading books on psychology.

    DSHBRD

    An interactive, customizable virtual social assistant. The driving concept behind this project was that there is now an app for everything a person can think of, but a user needs to have so many different apps downloaded in order to get all the information he or she needs. DSHBRD is meant to save you time by keeping everything in one place.
    Front-End built with React.js, React Router, powered by an Express.js Back-End connected to a PostgreSQL database.
